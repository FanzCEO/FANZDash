## Overview

Age verification is **required by law** in multiple jurisdictions to ensure that only adults (18+) can access adult content. This guide explains why verification is required, how it works, and step-by-step instructions for each verification method.

Age verification is **mandatory** in these locations:

### üá¨üáß United Kingdom

- Various member state regulations
- Part of broader child protection framework

**If you're in one of these locations, you MUST verify your age before accessing adult content.**

## üåç How Location Detection Works

When you first access Fanz Dash:

1. **IP Geolocation Check**
   - System detects your IP address
   - Determines your country and state/region
   - Checks if age verification is required in your location

2. **Age Verification Gate**
   - If required: Age verification modal appears
   - If not required: No verification needed (but recommended)

3. **Verification Status Stored**
   - Once verified, stored for future sessions
   - No need to reverify on subsequent visits
   - Stored for 7 years (federal law requirement)

**Note:** Using VPN/proxy to bypass verification is a violation of Terms of Service and may result in permanent ban.

## Method 1: Third-Party AVS (VerifyMy) ‚≠ê **RECOMMENDED**

**Best for:** Most users
**Time:** 5-10 minutes
**Cost:** $0.50-$1.50 (charged by VerifyMy)
**Privacy:** High - No ID stored on Fanz servers

### How It Works

VerifyMy is a third-party age verification service that specializes in privacy-focused age verification. Fanz Dash never receives or stores your ID documents - only the verification result.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Click "Verify with VerifyMy"**
   - Age verification gate will appear
   - Click "VerifyMy Age Verification" button

2. **Redirected to VerifyMy**
   - Opens in new window/tab
   - You'll see VerifyMy's secure verification page

3. **Choose Verification Type**
   - **Option A: Government ID**
     - Upload photo of ID (driver's license, passport, national ID)
     - Supported: US Driver's License, Passport, EU National ID, UK Driving License
   - **Option B: Credit/Debit Card**
     - Enter card details (temporary $0.01 authorization)
     - Card must be in your name
   - **Option C: Digital Wallet**
     - Connect Apple Pay, Google Pay, or similar
     - Instant verification

4. **Take Selfie** (For ID verification)
   - VerifyMy will ask for a live selfie
   - Used to match face on ID
   - Not stored after verification

5. **Payment**
   - $0.50-$1.50 verification fee
   - One-time charge
   - No recurring fees

6. **Verification Result**
   - VerifyMy processes (30 seconds - 5 minutes)
   - Result sent to Fanz Dash
   - You're automatically redirected back
   - Access granted immediately

### What Fanz Stores

- ‚úÖ Verification result (passed/failed)
- ‚úÖ Verification date and time
- ‚úÖ VerifyMy transaction ID
- ‚úÖ Your age (18+, not exact birthdate)
- ‚ùå **NOT STORED:** ID documents, photos, personal details

### Privacy Protection

- **End-to-end encryption** during transmission
- **GDPR compliant** (EU data protection)
- **CCPA compliant** (California privacy law)
- **PCI DSS Level 1** (highest payment security standard)

### Supported ID Documents

**United States:**
- Driver's License (all 50 states)
- Passport
- State ID Card
- Military ID
- Tribal ID

**United Kingdom:**
- UK Passport
- UK Driving License
- National Identity Card
- Biometric Residence Permit

**European Union:**
- EU National ID Cards
- EU Passports
- Residence Permits

**Other Countries:**
- Check VerifyMy's supported documents list during verification

### Troubleshooting

**"ID not recognized"**
- Ensure photo is clear and well-lit
- All 4 corners of ID must be visible
- No glare or shadows
- Try a different ID document

**"Selfie doesn't match"**
- Remove glasses/hats
- Ensure good lighting
- Face camera directly

**"Payment failed"**
- Try different payment method
- Check card has international transactions enabled
- Use alternative verification method

## Method 2: ID Document Upload

**Best for:** Users without payment method
**Time:** 10-15 minutes
**Cost:** Free
**Privacy:** Medium - ID uploaded to Fanz servers (encrypted)

### How It Works

Upload a photo of your government-issued ID directly to Fanz. Human moderators verify the document and your age. ID is encrypted and stored for 7 years (federal law requirement).

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Click "Upload ID Document"**
   - Age verification gate will appear
   - Click "Upload ID Document" button

2. **Choose Document Type**
   - Driver's License
   - Passport
   - National ID Card
   - State/Government ID

3. **Take Photos**
   - **Front of ID:** Clear photo showing all details
   - **Back of ID:** (if applicable)
   - **Selfie with ID:** Hold ID next to your face

4. **Photo Requirements**
   - Well-lit (natural light preferred)
   - All 4 corners visible
   - Text clearly readable
   - No glare or shadows
   - Minimum 1280x720 pixels

5. **Submit for Review**
   - Click "Submit for Verification"
   - Human moderator reviews within 24 hours
   - You'll receive email notification when complete

6. **Verification Result**
   - **Approved:** Access granted immediately
   - **Rejected:** Email explains reason, can resubmit

### What We Store

- ‚úÖ Front and back photos of ID (encrypted)
- ‚úÖ Selfie with ID (encrypted)
- ‚úÖ Your name (as shown on ID)
- ‚úÖ Date of birth
- ‚úÖ ID number (partially redacted: XXX-XX-1234)
- ‚úÖ Issue and expiration dates

**Storage:** 7 years (required by 18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2257)

**Encryption:** AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit

### Privacy & Security

- **Encrypted storage** - Cannot be accessed without decryption key
- **Limited access** - Only verified compliance staff can view
- **Audit logging** - All access logged and monitored
- **Cannot be deleted** - Federal law requires 7-year retention

### Accepted ID Documents

**Must be:**
- ‚úÖ Government-issued
- ‚úÖ Current (not expired)
- ‚úÖ Shows date of birth
- ‚úÖ Shows photo
- ‚úÖ In Latin alphabet (or translated)

**Accepted:**
- Driver's License (any country)
- Passport (any country)
- National ID Card
- State/Provincial ID
- Military ID
- Permanent Resident Card
- Refugee Travel Document

**NOT Accepted:**
- ‚ùå School ID
- ‚ùå Work ID
- ‚ùå Social Security Card
- ‚ùå Birth Certificate (no photo)
- ‚ùå Expired documents

### Troubleshooting

**"Document unclear or unreadable"**
- Retake photo in better lighting
- Ensure all text is sharp and readable
- Avoid glare from flash or windows
- Use higher resolution camera if possible

**"Selfie doesn't match ID photo"**
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Face camera directly
- Remove glasses/hats (match ID photo)
- If you look significantly different from ID photo (weight change, facial hair, etc.), include explanation note

**"Document expired"**
- You must have a current, non-expired ID
- If your ID recently expired, renew it first
- Cannot accept expired documents (federal law)

**"Review taking longer than 24 hours"**
- High volume may cause delays
- Check your spam folder for verification email
- Contact support@fanz.com if 48+ hours

## Method 3: Credit Card Verification

**Best for:** Quick verification
**Time:** 2-3 minutes
**Cost:** Free (temporary $0.01 authorization, refunded)
**Privacy:** Medium - Card details processed, not stored

### How It Works

A temporary $0.01 authorization is placed on your credit or debit card. Because credit cards are only issued to adults (18+), this confirms your age. The charge is immediately refunded.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Click "Verify with Credit Card"**
   - Age verification gate will appear
   - Click "Credit Card Verification" button

2. **Enter Card Details**
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV/CVC code
   - Billing ZIP code

3. **Supported Cards**
   - Visa
   - Mastercard
   - American Express
   - Discover
   - Debit cards with major card network

4. **Authorization Charge**
   - $0.01 temporary authorization placed on card
   - You'll see "FANZ AGE VERIFICATION" on statement
   - Refunded within 1-5 business days

5. **Instant Verification**
   - If card processes successfully: Access granted immediately
   - Card details verified, you're 18+
   - No ongoing charges

### What We Store

- ‚úÖ Last 4 digits of card number
- ‚úÖ Card type (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
- ‚úÖ Expiration date (month/year only)
- ‚úÖ Verification timestamp
- ‚ùå **NOT STORED:** Full card number, CVV, billing address

### Privacy & Security

- **PCI DSS Compliant** - Highest payment security standard
- **Tokenization** - Card number replaced with secure token
- **No storage of full card details** - Only last 4 digits kept
- **TLS 1.3 encryption** - All data encrypted in transit

### Requirements

- **Must be in your name** - Card must belong to you
- **Must be valid** - Not expired or cancelled
- **Must have funds** - At least $0.01 available
- **International transactions enabled** - Some banks block by default

### Troubleshooting

**"Card declined"**
- Check card is not expired
- Verify CVV is correct
- Ensure card has at least $0.01 available
- Contact your bank (may be blocking transaction)
- Try different card

**"This card has already been used"**
- Each card can only verify one account
- Use a different card
- If you previously verified another account, contact support

**"Card verification failed"**
- Bank may have blocked transaction
- Enable international transactions
- Try alternative verification method

## Method 4: AI Age Estimation

**Best for:** Privacy-conscious users
**Time:** 1-2 minutes
**Cost:** Free
**Privacy:** Highest - No ID or payment info needed

### How It Works

AI analyzes a live photo of your face to estimate your age. If the AI determines you appear 18+, verification passes. This method is privacy-focused - no ID documents, no payment info, no personal details.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Click "AI Age Estimation"**
   - Age verification gate will appear
   - Click "AI Age Estimation" button

2. **Grant Camera Permission**
   - Browser will ask for camera access
   - Click "Allow"
   - Needed for live photo capture

3. **Take Live Photo**
   - Face camera directly
   - Remove glasses, hats, masks
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Follow on-screen instructions

4. **AI Analysis**
   - AI processes your photo (5-10 seconds)
   - Estimates your age based on facial features
   - Determines if you appear 18+

5. **Verification Result**
   - **Pass:** AI determines you appear 18+ ‚Üí Access granted
   - **Fail:** AI determines you appear under 18 ‚Üí Try different method
   - **Unclear:** AI cannot determine ‚Üí Try different method

### What We Store

- ‚úÖ Photo of your face (encrypted)
- ‚úÖ AI age estimate (e.g., "appears 25-30")
- ‚úÖ Verification timestamp
- ‚ùå **NOT STORED:** Name, ID, payment info, personal details

**Storage:** 7 years (federal law requirement)

### AI Technology

- **Model:** Proprietary age estimation CNN (Convolutional Neural Network)
- **Training:** Trained on 10M+ diverse faces across all ages, ethnicities
- **Accuracy:** 95%+ accuracy for ages 18-65
- **Bias Testing:** Regular audits for demographic fairness

### Privacy Protection

- **No PII required** - Photo of face only
- **Encrypted storage** - Photo encrypted at rest
- **Not used for training** - Your photo never used to train AI
- **Cannot be linked to identity** - No name or ID associated

### Limitations

- **May not work for everyone** - Some facial features harder to estimate
- **Edge cases** - Very young-looking adults (18-22) may fail
- **Appearance changes** - Significant weight loss/gain, surgery may affect
- **Makeup/filters** - May interfere with estimation

**If AI verification fails, you can immediately try a different method.**

### Troubleshooting

**"Could not estimate age"**
- Ensure good, even lighting
- Face camera directly (not angled)
- Remove glasses, hats, sunglasses
- No makeup or filters
- Try again (up to 3 attempts)

**"Verification failed - appear under 18"**
- If you ARE 18+, try different verification method
- AI may have difficulty with your facial features
- Use ID upload or VerifyMy for certain approval

**"Camera not working"**
- Check browser permissions (camera access)
- Try different browser
- Ensure camera is not in use by another app
- Try on mobile device if desktop camera not working

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to verify on every device?
No. Verification is account-based, not device-based. Once verified, you're verified on all devices.

### Can I use someone else's ID?
**No. This is identity fraud and is illegal.** You must use your own ID.

### What if I don't have a government ID?
- Try **Credit Card** method (any card in your name)
- Try **AI Age Estimation** (no ID needed)
- Contact support@fanz.com for alternative options

### Can I verify if I'm under 18?
**No. You must be 18 or older to use Fanz.** If you're under 18, you cannot access the platform, even with parental permission.

### What if verification fails?
- Try a different verification method
- Check troubleshooting section for your method
- Contact support@fanz.com for assistance

### Is my verification data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted, access-controlled, and stored on secure servers. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and PCI DSS standards.

### Can I delete my verification data?
**No, not for 7 years.** Federal law (18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2257) requires retention for 7 years. After 7 years, data is automatically purged.

### Why does verification cost money (VerifyMy)?
Fanz doesn't charge for verification - the $0.50-$1.50 fee is charged by VerifyMy (the third-party verification provider) to cover their operational costs. **All other methods are free.**

### What happens if I move to a different country/state?
Your verification remains valid. No need to reverify when relocating.

## üö® Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: "Age verification required" even though I'm in a non-required location

**Causes:**
- VPN/proxy detected
- IP geolocation detected you as in required jurisdiction
- Account settings specify required jurisdiction

**Solutions:**
1. Disable VPN/proxy
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Contact support with your actual location
4. Verify anyway (recommended for all users)

---

### Issue: All verification methods failing

**Causes:**
- Account flagged for suspicious activity
- Multiple failed attempts triggered cooldown
- Technical issue with verification service

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 24 hours and try again
2. Try different method
3. Contact support@fanz.com with:
   - Username
   - Methods attempted
   - Error messages received
   - Screenshots (if applicable)

---

### Issue: "This account has already been verified"

**Explanation:** Your account is already verified. This message appears when you try to reverify.

**Action:** No action needed. You have full access.

- Fanz will honor VerifyMy's decision

**For Credit Card:**
- $0.01 charge is temporary and will be refunded
- If verification failed for other reasons, try different method
- Refund processes within 1-5 business days
