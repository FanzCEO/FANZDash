## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if content is obviously roleplay/fantasy?
**A:** Payment processors don't distinguish between real and fantasy for prohibited categories. Even consensual roleplay of incest, non-consent, or minors is prohibited.

### Q: Can I use "barely legal" in my title if the performer is 18+?
**A:** No. Payment processors flag "barely legal" and similar terms as high-risk, even if everyone is 18+.

### Q: What about cartoon/anime content?
**A:** Cartoons depicting minors sexually are illegal under PROTECT Act 2003. Adult cartoon content must depict clearly adult characters.

### Q: Are there any exceptions to the rules?
**A:** No. Prohibited content rules apply to everyone equally. No exceptions for:
- Professional producers
- Verified performers
- Paid subscribers
- Private content

### Q: What if I blur/censor prohibited content?
**A:** Blurring or censoring does not make prohibited content compliant. If the underlying content violates policy, it's still prohibited.

### Q: Can I appeal a content removal?
**A:** Yes. Use the Compliance Dashboard to file an appeal. Include:
- Explanation of why content should be allowed
- Evidence content complies with policies
- Response within 24-48 hours (EU users: 14 days under DSA)

### Q: What happens to my earnings if I'm banned?
**A:**
- **Warnings/temporary suspension:** Payments processed after suspension ends
- **Permanent ban:** Remaining balance paid out (minus violations), then account closed
- **Criminal violations (CSAM, trafficking):** All funds may be frozen for law enforcement
