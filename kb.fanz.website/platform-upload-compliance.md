## Overview

Fanz Dash offers automated content upload to external platforms (OnlyFans, Fansly, Patreon, etc.). Before content is uploaded to external platforms, it **must pass comprehensive compliance screening** including CSAM detection, payment processor checks, and trafficking assessments.

This guide explains how external platform compliance works, platform-specific requirements, and best practices for successful uploads.

---

## Why Platform Upload Compliance Matters

### Your Reputation is at Stake

**Getting banned from external platforms can destroy your creator career:**

1. **Permanent Bans**
   - Most platforms permanently ban for serious violations
   - Ban includes payment processing blacklist
   - Cannot create new account (tracked by ID, payment, device)

2. **Payment Processor Termination**
   - Visa/Mastercard violations follow you across all platforms
   - If OnlyFans bans you for payment processor violation, other platforms will too
   - Payment processing blacklists are shared industry-wide

3. **Financial Loss**
   - Lose all subscribers on banned platform
   - Outstanding payments may be forfeited
   - Rebuilding audience on new platform takes months/years

4. **Legal Consequences**
   - Serious violations (CSAM, trafficking) result in criminal charges
   - Federal law enforcement can prosecute across all platforms
   - Fines and imprisonment possible

**Fanz Dash's compliance screening protects you from accidental violations.**

1. Third-party management mentions

3. Multiple performers in single account
4. Pricing anomalies (suspiciously low)

## Stage 3: Risk Assessment & Upload Decision

### Per-Platform Risk Scoring

**Fanz Dash calculates separate risk scores for each platform:**

**Risk Factors:**

1. **Content Analysis** (50% of score)
   - Prohibited categories for specific platform
   - Keyword detection in captions/descriptions
   - Visual content analysis (AI)

2. **Historical Performance** (25% of score)
   - Previous violations on that platform
   - False positives (content approved by platform after manual review)
   - Removal rate (what % of uploads get removed by platform)

3. **Platform Strictness** (15% of score)
   - OnlyFans: Higher base risk (strict enforcement)
   - Fansly: Medium base risk
   - ManyVids: Lower base risk (permissive)

4. **Account Standing** (10% of score)
   - Current warnings/restrictions on platform
   - Account age (new accounts higher risk)
   - Subscriber count (high-performing accounts get some leniency)

### Manual Review Process

**When Your Upload Requires Review:**

1. **Notification Sent**
   - Email: "Your upload to [Platform] requires manual review"
   - Expected review time: 2-24 hours
   - Content held, not uploaded yet

2. **Compliance Team Reviews**
   - Human moderator watches content
   - Checks against platform-specific rules
   - Makes final determination: Approve or block

3. **Possible Outcomes**

   **A) Approved**
   - Content uploaded to platform(s)
   - You receive confirmation email
   - Risk score adjusted (future similar content may auto-approve)

   **B) Approved with Changes**
   - Content okay, but caption/title needs changes
   - Moderator suggests edits
   - You approve edits ‚Üí upload proceeds

   **C) Blocked**
   - Content violates platform rules
   - Email explains specific violation
   - Content NOT uploaded
   - Can appeal decision

4. **Appeal Process**
   - Reply to notification email with explanation
   - Senior compliance officer reviews
   - Final decision within 48 hours

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Upload Blocked - High Risk Score

**Why it happens:**
- Content flagged for prohibited categories
- Keyword detected in caption/title
- High platform-specific risk (e.g., OnlyFans strict on certain content)

**What to do:**

1. **Check Notification Email**
   - Specific reason provided
   - Suggests changes (e.g., "Remove 'daddy' from caption")

2. **Make Changes**
   - Edit caption/description
   - Re-crop video if background issue
   - Change title/tags

3. **Resubmit**
   - Upload again with changes
   - Usually auto-approves after compliance edits

4. **If Still Blocked**
   - Contact support@fanz.com
   - May require manual review
   - Consider uploading to different platform (less strict)

---

### Issue: Content Uploaded but Removed by External Platform

**Why it happens:**
- Platform's AI flagged content after upload
- User reported content
- Manual platform review determined violation
- False positive (platform AI error)

**What to do:**

1. **Check Platform Notification**
   - Platform emails violation notice
   - Read specific reason

2. **Determine if Valid Violation**
   - **Valid:** Content actually violates platform rules
     - Don't re-upload same content
     - Update Fanz Dash settings to avoid future similar uploads
     - Consider different platform for this content type

   - **False Positive:** Content doesn't violate rules
     - Appeal to platform directly (use platform's appeal process)
     - Document appeal in Fanz Dash (helps future risk scoring)
     - If appeal denied, do not upload similar content again

3. **Update Fanz Dash**
   - Fanz Dash automatically detects removal
   - Risk score increased for similar content
   - You're alerted for future uploads of similar type

---

### Issue: Manual Review Taking Too Long

**Expected review times:**
- Standard: 2-12 hours
- High volume periods (Friday-Sunday): 12-24 hours
- Urgent review needed: Contact support

**What to do:**

1. **Check Status**
   - Dashboard: Compliance ‚Üí Pending Reviews
   - Shows estimated review completion time

2. **If Urgent**
   - Email: compliance@fanz.com
   - Subject: "Urgent Review Request - [Upload ID]"
   - Explain urgency (e.g., scheduled post, time-sensitive promo)

3. **Expedited Review**
   - Available for verified creators with good standing
   - $25 expedite fee (reviewed within 1 hour)
   - Option presented during upload for medium-risk content

---

### Issue: All Performers Verified but Upload Still Blocked

**Why it happens:**
- Performer appears under 18 (AI age estimation false positive)
- Performer's verification expired (reverify every 12 months)
- New performer not added to this specific upload

**What to do:**

1. **Check Performer Verification Status**
   - Dashboard ‚Üí Performers
   - Ensure all performers verified within last 12 months
   - Check expiration dates

2. **Re-Verify if Needed**
   - Performers must re-verify every year
   - Send re-verification request via dashboard
   - Upload held until re-verified

### Issue: OnlyFans Removed Content but It's Not Against Rules

**OnlyFans false positive (common issue):**

**Common False Positive Scenarios:**

   - OnlyFans may remove anyway (appearance-based)

3. **Background Item Flagged**
   - Weapon or drug paraphernalia visible in background
   - Not focus of content, but OnlyFans flags it
   - Appeal: Explain item not part of content, remove and re-upload

**How to Appeal to OnlyFans:**

1. **OnlyFans Appeals Process**
   - Email: complaints@onlyfans.com
   - Include: Content URL, explanation, evidence (verification docs)
   - Response time: 3-5 business days

2. **If Appeal Denied**
   - Do NOT re-upload same content (risk permanent ban)
   - Modify content (remove flagged element) and re-upload
   - Or upload to different platform (Fansly less strict)

3. **Update Fanz Dash**
   - Document removal and appeal result
   - Future similar content will be flagged for review before OnlyFans upload
   - May auto-upload to other platforms but hold OnlyFans

## FAQ for Creators

### Do I need to manually upload to each platform, or does Fanz Dash do it?

**Fanz Dash does it automatically.** You upload once to Fanz Dash, select which platforms, and Fanz uploads to all selected platforms. You receive confirmation for each successful upload.

---

### Can I edit content after it's uploaded to external platforms?

**No, not directly through Fanz Dash.** Once uploaded to external platforms, edits must be made on the platform itself. You can re-upload a new version through Fanz Dash, but the original post remains on the platform until you manually delete it.

---

### What if I want to upload to OnlyFans myself (not through Fanz)?

**You can do both.** Fanz Dash links to your external accounts but doesn't prevent manual uploads. However, ALL content uploaded through Fanz (even if you later manually upload elsewhere) is screened for compliance.

**Recommendation:** Use Fanz Dash for all uploads to benefit from compliance screening (protects you from accidental violations).

---

### If content is blocked for OnlyFans, can I still upload to Fansly?

**Yes.** Fanz Dash assesses risk separately for each platform. Content blocked for OnlyFans (strict) may be approved for Fansly (less strict) or ManyVids (most permissive).

---

### How do I know if my content was removed from an external platform?

**Fanz Dash alerts you:**
- Email notification when content removed
- Dashboard shows removal status
- Reason provided (if platform specifies)

**Check daily:** Dashboard ‚Üí External Platforms ‚Üí Removed Content

---

### Can I appeal if Fanz Dash blocks my upload?

**Yes.** Reply to the block notification email with your explanation. Senior compliance officer reviews within 48 hours. If appeal approved, content is uploaded. If denied, explanation provided.

---

### Does Fanz Dash charge extra for multi-platform uploads?

**No additional fees.** Multi-platform upload included in Fanz Dash subscription. You only pay external platform fees (e.g., OnlyFans 20% commission).

---

### What happens if I get banned from OnlyFans? Can I still use Fanz Dash?

**Yes.** Fanz Dash supports multiple platforms. If banned from one platform, you can continue uploading to others. Fanz Dash can help you transition to alternative platforms.
